Our 4-Step OEM Process

01

3D Design & Simulation

Our engineering team creates detailed 3D models using UG/SolidWorks. We perform vacuum retention analysis to ensure every part handles heavy-duty loads of 50kg+. Design for Manufacturing (DFM) optimization begins immediately, identifying cost-saving opportunities before tooling begins.

02

Rapid Tooling & Sampling

With 11 CNC machines and 5 EDM sets in-house, we produce tooling 30% faster than outsourced options. Initial samples are ready in 7-10 days. Every iteration is tested against your specifications, with mold modifications completed instantly based on test results.

03

Custom Rubber Formulation

Our 20+ years of rubber expertise allows us to develop custom compounds for extreme conditions. Whether you need temperature resistance from -22°F to 248°F or specialized durometer ratings, our chemists create the perfect formulation for your application.

04

Full QC & FBA Packaging

Every unit undergoes 48-hour vacuum testing. We offer Amazon-ready retail packaging and handle all logistics including FOB and DDP shipping to global destinations. Quality documentation and third-party test reports available upon request.
